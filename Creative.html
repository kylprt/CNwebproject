Odd's End

A weird fiction/cosmic horror TTRPG (Tabletop Role Playing Game) drawing inspiration from Call of Cthulu, Tales From the Loop, and similarly themed podcasts such as Welcome to Night Vale. Set in the rural township of Odd's End, nestled amongst mist-topped mountains and surrounded by ancient andforeboding pine forests, players find themselves confronted by bizarre occurences, unnatural events and otherworldy creatures. Taking on the role of one of the town's many lost souls, players must use their skills and mysterious artefacts called 'oddments' to solve problems, find answers, and even defend themselves.
